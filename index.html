Welcome To The Short Rest Café

Welcome weary traveler, we were once adventurer's like you...until we took an arrow to the knee.

The Short Rest Café was established in 2026, and we are a small family friendly environment, with a nice and cosy ambience perfect for taking a breather and relaxing whilst sampling our exquisite drinks and delectable pastries.

The theming of our establishment takes inspiration from numerous fantasy works. Like Dungeons & Dragons and The Lord of The Rings, we have made it our own and enjoyed putting our own spin on things, to create a comforting and safe feeling space for any fellow adventurer to enjoy to their hearts content. We hope you stop and stay for a while.

We look forward to see you upon your next visit!
